The beautiful inn at Serenity Central of New England can house up to 32 retreat participants for overnight stays. And the Great Room and Dining area will comfortably accommodate that same maximum number. During 2024, the typical retreats served 15-25 participants.


Throughout the 2024 year, Serenity Central was pleased to provide accommodations for: multiple AA groups, meditation and yoga retreats, religious youth groups, multiple family events, and regular meditation sessions for local community members.


Plans for 2025 include expansion of the range of events to host local community interest groups, training sessions for a variety of service groups, and special topic retreats, such as A Course in Miracles, The Presence Process, and Out of the Matrix retreats, based on next generation Course of Miracles concepts and principles.
