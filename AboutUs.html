Mission

Offering quality experiences which inspire spiritual growth, spiritual healing and personal
well-being in a judgement free, safe, pan denominational environment. Loving-kindness is
a central focus of the retreat center’s work.

Vision and Goals

To be the premier spiritual retreat center in New England.

To meet the needs of persons seeking emotional sobriety and stability, those seeking to
actualize a spiritual renewal, and those searching for pathways to increased spiritual awareness.
