Our Mission

Offering quality experiences which inspire spiritual growth, healing, and personal well-being in a judgment-free, safe, pan-denominational environment. Loving-kindness is central to the retreat center’s work.

Vision and Goals

To be the premier spiritual retreat center in New England.

To meet the needs of individuals seeking emotional sobriety and stability, spiritual renewal, and pathways to enhanced spiritual awareness.
